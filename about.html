OUR STORY

Our story started with TPE class and a simple question: "What is the one thing that you would do if you had all the time in the world?"
And the answer was simple: "Play".
And so, we set out to create a platform that would help people find and organize sports activities in their local communities.

We are a team of 3 students from IIIT Hyderabad, who are passionate about sports and believe that sports can be a great way to bring people together.

We warmly welcome you to sportsverse and hope that you find it useful in your journey to find and organize sports activities in your local communities.
